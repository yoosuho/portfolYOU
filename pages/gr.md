<h2>üîß Project Overview</h2>
<p>
This national project focused on electrifying the PTO (Power Take-Off) systems used in special-purpose commercial vehicles such as cranes, lifters, and refrigerated trucks. Traditional PTOs, powered by internal combustion engines, suffered from poor efficiency, frequent idling, and emissions. This project introduced a multi-channel, battery-powered electric alternative.
</p>
