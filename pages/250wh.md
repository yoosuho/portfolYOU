<p>
This project, funded at KRW 6.363 billion (approx. USD 4.8 million), aimed to break through the performance limitations of existing lithium battery modules used in electric aviation and hydrogen-electric vehicles. The mission was to develop a battery system that delivers both high energy density (250‚ÄØWh/kg) and high discharge capability (80‚ÄØC peak), while passing all safety and environmental standards applicable to advanced transportation platforms.

</p>

<h2>My Contributions</h2>
